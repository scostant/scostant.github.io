hi there, sugar

bay area beans was founded in 1970 by barb hudson. this bay area native always had a love for coffee, and she wanted to share her love with the rest of san francisco. barb had a goal of making all of the beverages and food with organic ingredients. she wanted to have a spot where all young people could meet for coffee in the city. come to bay area beans and experience some sf culture!
